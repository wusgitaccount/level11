Poster Series

My body of work titled Consumption Programme revolves around the idea of addiction and consumerism, and is a series of 4 A1 posters and 1 A5 publication. I’ve mainly followed the process of constantly collecting loyalty stamp cards and making scans of each one. The style of my project is in full black and white and involves a lot of collaging methods as I’ve intended this body of work to capture a very inky, rustic feel. Local cafés and restaurants that attract and build brand loyalty with customers by using these stamp cards is the field that I have researched on; this is further shown in the mini publication that includes articles on daily food/drink consumption and the strategy of the loyalty card.
